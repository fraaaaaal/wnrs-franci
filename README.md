# We're Not Really Strangers — Franci Edition 🃏

> *"Either you'll leave knowing everything about each other — or you'll leave 
> knowing too much. Either way, no one is walking out of this the same."*

---

## What is this?

213 cards. Zero small talk. Good luck.

A digital version of the cult card game *We're Not Really Strangers*, 
personally curated and expanded by Franci. Three levels designed to take 
you from first impressions to your deepest fears — with wildcards that will 
make you laugh, cringe, and overshare in equal measure.

Therapy was too expensive so we made a card game instead.

---

## How to play

1. Open the link
2. Press **Draw a card**
3. Answer honestly
4. Try not to fall in love

---

## Levels

| Level | Theme | Vibe |
|-------|-------|------|
| 01 — Perception | First impressions & assumptions | Light, fun, a little spicy |
| 02 — Connection | The rarely asked questions | Deep, real, no hiding |
| 03 — Reflection | What you've learned about each other | Vulnerable, beautiful, heavy |
| Wildcards | Chaos | Unpredictable |

---

## Features

- 213 unique cards across 4 categories
- Never repeats a card in the same session
- Filter by level to play in order
- Skip a card and come back to it later
- Works on iPhone, tablet, desktop — any device, any browser
- Fully offline once loaded
